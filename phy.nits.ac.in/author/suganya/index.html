About Suganya Devi K

I am working as an Assistant Professor Grade-I , Dept of CSE, National Institute Of Technology Silchar,Cachar, Assam, India. I have completed my Ph.D. in 2014 and M.E. Multimedia Technology (Gold Medalist) in 2008 at Anna University, Chennai, Tamilnadu. My area of research includes Image Processing, Medical Imaging, Video Segmentation, Cloud computing and Security, Multimedia. I have published more than 35 research papers in various reputed International journals and International Conferences with high Impact factor.
