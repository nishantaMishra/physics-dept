Biographical Skectch

Dr. P. Srinivasan was born in Neyveli Township, Tamilnadu in the year 1980. He received his B.Sc and M.Sc ( Applied Physics) from Bharathidasan University , Tiruchirappalli in the year 2000 and 2002 respectively. He received M.Phil (Physics) from M.S University Tirunelveli during 2003 and Ph.D degree from Anna University, Chennai in the year 2008. He was a post doctoral fellow at Dept of Optoelectronics, University of Kerala in the year 2008. He worked as Assistant professor at Anna University Panruti Campus from Sep 2008 till Oct 2018.
